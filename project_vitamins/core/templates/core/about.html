About VitaminSHE

Empowering women through personalized vitamin and nutrition guidance

Our Mission

VitaminSHE was created with a simple mission: to empower women to take control of their health through personalized vitamin tracking, evidence-based nutrition recommendations, and access to trusted medical resources. We believe that every woman deserves easy access to the tools and knowledge needed to optimize their nutritional health.

Our Story

The Problem

Women face unique nutritional challenges including pregnancy, menopause, hormonal fluctuations, and specific micronutrient deficiencies. Yet, most health tracking apps treat everyone the same. There's no easy way for users to:

  • Track multiple vitamins and minerals consistently
  • Get personalized recommendations based on their health profile
  • Access trustworthy, medically-reviewed nutritional information
  • Find nearby clinics for blood tests to confirm deficiencies

Our Solution

VitaminSHE combines four powerful features into one integrated platform:

  • Smart Tracking: Log intake of 12 essential vitamins with multiple sources and units
  • AI Recommendations: Rule-based engine that analyzes health profile and suggests personalized vitamins
  • Health Resources: Curated library of medical articles and evidence-based information
  • Clinic Locator: Find nearby blood testing centers and diagnostic labs using integrated maps

The Impact

By making vitamin tracking and health knowledge accessible, VitaminSHE helps women:

  • Identify potential vitamin deficiencies early
  • Make informed decisions about supplementation
  • Maintain consistent health tracking habits
  • Access medical resources when needed
  • Improve overall wellness and quality of life

Core Features

📊 Vitamin Tracking

Log your daily intake of 12 essential vitamins from various sources. Track adherence and identify patterns in your nutrition.

🧬 Smart Recommendations

AI-powered engine that analyzes your health profile and suggests personalized vitamin recommendations based on your specific needs.

📚 Health Resources

Access hundreds of medically-reviewed articles about vitamins, minerals, nutrition, and health conditions from trusted sources.

📍 Clinic Locator

Find nearby blood testing centers, diagnostic labs, and medical clinics using our integrated Google Maps locator.

📈 Health Analytics

View detailed dashboards showing your vitamin intake patterns, adherence rates, and current health streaks.

🔐 Private & Secure

Your health data is encrypted and protected. User privacy and security are our top priorities.
